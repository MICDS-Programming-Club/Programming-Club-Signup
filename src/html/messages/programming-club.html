Opportunities

  • Drones

    We finally got our Crazyflie drones working, which we can use for our own malicious completely educational purposes! You may have seen these little guys flying around at Activities Fair! Make software that communicates with each drone to plan flight formations and interact with external input.

  • Web Development

    The internet is one of the most powerful innovations we have at our fingertips. Leverage this amazing space and share your own ideas through the power of programming. This includes maintaining the student-run site, MyMICDS.net: a place to view your schedule, lunch, homework, bulletin, and more!

  • Videogame Development

    What's a more productive waste of your time than making videogames? Using game engines like Unity or Unreal Engine, make your ideas come to life.

  • WWT Hackathon

    Each year, a company called World Wide Technology (WWT) invites high schools in the Saint Louis area to a four-month-long competition to engineer a solution that addresses a problem in our community. Last year, the MICDS team won first place and $10,000 for our school!

  • Slack Bots

    One of the most exciting parts of technology is interfacing it with humans. Make a chat bot that communicates via Slack to do anything you want from holding conversations to giving a summary of a Wikipedia page.

If you have any other project ideas the Programming Club can do, contact one of the co-heads or sugguest it in the Slack group!

How to Learn

Programming Club has a compilation of guides located on a site called GitHub which are aimed to get beginners started. You will need to learn different languages depending on what you want to do. For example, web development requires knowledge of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. There's a whole list of resources available on the Programming Club Guides, but here's a few websites to get you started:

  • Codecademy

    Codecademy has multiple courses taught through interactive coding challenges. It's completely free!

  • Treehouse

    Treehouse offers a variety of courses taught by experts on web design, coding, business, and much more. Watch a series of videos and take quizzes and coding challenges afterwards.
